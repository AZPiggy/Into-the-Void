
Forest Adventure

Welcome to Forest Adventure, a whimsical 2D platformer where the trees are tall, the bees are angry, and even the coins are little red creatures.

How to Play: 
You are a brave explorer on a quest through the mysterious forest. Your job? Collect as many coins as possible, avoid grumpy forest dwellers, and reach the ancient house at the end of each level.

Oh — did we mention the coins are alive?

Controls:
- Move: A / D or ← / →
- Jump: Space
- Start Game: S
- Restart: R (on Game Over or Victory screen)

The Creatures (a.k.a. Things That Move)

Coins:
They’re kinda cute.
Jump on them to collect them and gain +1 point.
Just don’t get too attached... they disappear when you step on them.

Bees:
Fast, furious, and allergic to hugs.
Touching one will sting your pride (and your health).

Boars:
They charge. They don’t stop.
They’re like angry potatoes with legs.
One touch = instant doom. Respect the bacon.

Dying (It Happens):
- Fall off the world? Gone.
- Boar collision? Toast.
- Bee cuddle? Pain.
- Run out of time? Uh oh.

But hey — you’ll look very dramatic doing it (yes, we gave you a death animation).

Winning:
Make it to the house at the end of the level to win!
You’ll hear a triumphant sound, and you'll get that warm fuzzy feeling of victory. Probably.
